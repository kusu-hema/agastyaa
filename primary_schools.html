Welcome to Agastyaa Primary School!

Agastyaa Primary School is dedicated to providing a nurturing and inspiring learning environment where young minds can flourish and grow. Our primary school programs are designed to lay a strong foundation for academic success while fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a love for learning.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students to become curious, confident, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to excel in an ever-changing world. Through our Concept-Oriented Integrated Programmes, we strive to ignite a passion for learning, cultivate resilience, and instill a sense of responsibility to the community and the environment.

Transformative Education

At Agastyaa Primary School, we believe that every child has the potential to succeed. Our Concept-Oriented Integrated Programmes are designed to engage students in meaningful learning experiences that spark curiosity and imagination. With dedicated teachers and a supportive learning environment, we inspire students to explore, discover, and achieve their full potential.

Holistic Development

Our CO-School model emphasizes holistic development, nurturing not only academic excellence but also social-emotional skills, physical wellness, and character development. We offer a well-rounded curriculum that includes a range of extracurricular activities, arts, and sports to ensure that every child has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.

Empowering Young Learners

By choosing Agastyaa Primary School, parents are investing in their child's future. We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and supportive learning environment where every child feels valued and respected. Our goal is to empower young learners to become independent thinkers, lifelong learners, and responsible global citizens.

Join Us

Join us at Agastyaa Primary School and embark on an exciting educational journey filled with discovery, growth, and success. With our Concept-Oriented Integrated Programmes, students will develop the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to succeed in school and beyond. Take the first step towards a bright future by contacting us now to learn more about our programs and offerings. Together, let's inspire, empower, and transform the lives of our future leaders.
